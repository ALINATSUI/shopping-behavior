### 2.  63% of users that have purchased items on FlashFash have not left any kind of review ratings. This leaves a large gap in determining customer preferences and getting additional feedback organically. ###

### 3. Store pickups are also extremely low (only 2!) We want to encourage customers to shop in-store to get re-acquainted with the brand, share their experiences on social media and increase brand loyalty. Why are store pickups so low? Is it due to insufficient staffing, logistics, lack of demand in the area or something else? ###

### 4. If we compare the 0 count of previous purchases of new clients and compare to customers who've made at least two previous purchases, we're seeing a definite downward turn.
